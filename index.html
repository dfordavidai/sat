What is Satta King? – Complete Guide 2026

Satta King is India's most popular number-based lottery game. The word "Satta" means gambling or betting in Hindi, and "King" denotes the winner of the daily draw. Millions of players across India search for Satta King result today, Gali Disawar result, and Satta King fast updates on sites like sattaking-haryana.com every single day.

In Satta King 2026, players choose any number between 00 and 99, place their bet through a bookie or Satta King online platform, and wait for the official draw. If their number is selected, they win 90 times their bet amount. The four biggest markets are Gali, Disawar, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad — and all four results are published live on this page.

Satta King Haryana is a particularly strong search vertical. Haryana's flagship market, Faridabad Satta King, has been operating for decades and is synonymous with Haryana Satta. Players from Gurugram, Faridabad, Hisar, Rohtak, Karnal and across Haryana check this site for Satta King Haryana result daily.

Key Satta King Terms You Must Know

  • Satta King – The game itself; also the title given to the winning player
  • Satta King Result – The officially drawn winning number for each market
  • Gali Result – Nightly result from the Gali market (~11:30 PM)
  • Disawar Result – Morning result from the oldest market (~5:00 AM)
  • Faridabad Result – Evening result from Haryana's top market (~6:00 PM)
  • Ghaziabad Result – Night result from UP's top market (~8:30 PM)
  • Satta King Fast – Real-time, no-delay result delivery
  • Black Satta King – Popular alternate name for select Satta King variants
  • Satta King UP – Markets and players based in Uttar Pradesh
  • Satta King Haryana – Markets and players based in Haryana, led by Faridabad
  • Satta Matka – The Mumbai-origin predecessor game; terms often used interchangeably
  • Satta King Chart / Record Chart – Historical results calendar used for analysis
  • Satta Number – The chosen number a player bets on (00–99)
  • Satta Bazar – The organized marketplace where Satta King bets are placed
  • Desawar Result – Alternate spelling of Disawar, same market
