What is Satta King?

Satta King is a form of lottery-style betting game that originated in India. Players choose numbers and place bets, hoping their chosen number is selected in the final result.

How It Works

  • Select a number (0–99)
  • Place your bet
  • Wait for the result draw
  • If your number matches, you win
